Vehicle Solutions

Oversee and manage your enterprise fleet with AT&T IoT Platform. Collect and manage high volumes of data like location history, route paths, vehicle performance statistics, and safety. Leverage telematic data to make informed decisions, to save cost, schedule routes strategically, and grow your business effectively.

  • Application Logic & Hostin

    AT&T Flow allows you to quickly design and run Dockerized™ environments. Flow provides a single integration point to support mixed devices in your fleet by handling Protocol Translation. Whether you’re testing or ramping up for full-scale production, you’ll have the resources you need when you need them.

  • Time Series Data Storage

    Your enterprise’s fleet data will be highly-available when stored with AT&T's IoT Platform’s Time Series Data Store. Our cloud hosted infrastructure features redundant fail safes, and scalable architecture, ensuring your vehicle data is consistently available, safely secured with key-based authentication, and capable of ramping up quickly, allowing you to get back to the important aspects of growing your business.

  • Device Management

    Monitor your fleet’s status through M2X Device Management. Have a few vehicles you’d like to track within a specific geographic region? Easily organize your your device data into categories or group them together in a collection. Scale from a single device to millions with the click of a button using the Distribution tools.

You have the power to monitor your fleet of 100,000+ vehicles at your fingertips. By leveraging the AT&T Network, Flow and M2X, our solution provides you with the resources to optimize your business. Your fleet devices connect and communicate via AT&T’s Network, and then the device data is pushed through your AT&T Flow application environment to be normalized and sent to M2X for storage and processing.

In addition to M2X’s Data Store, Device data can be sent to a data center of your business’ choosing. Apart from the the robust scaling and reliability our platform provides for your fleet on a daily basis, Dashboard visualizations can be generated from the collected data points and used for analytics.
